Kerala

Kerala is situated on the southwestern tip of India. The state of Kerala is divided into hills and valleys, midland plains, and coastal belts. The backwaters situated along the coastal belts are one of the main tourist attractions of Kerala. Kerala is also popularly known as "God's Own Country" because of its breathtaking natural beauty and greenery.

Tamil Nadu

Tamil Nadu is situated in the southern part of India. It is bound by Andhra Pradesh and Karnataka in the north, the Indian Ocean in the south, the Bay of Bengal in the east, and Kerala in the west. Tamil Nadu is known for its chains of mountains, plateaus, residual hills, and coastal plains. People of Tamil Nadu are popular for their taste in music, dance, literature, and theatre.

Sikkim

Sikkim is situated in northeast India. It is bound by Tibet (China) in the north, West Bengal in the south, Tibet and Bhutan in the east, and Nepal in the west. Sikkim is known for its many glaciers, alpine meadows, and wildflowers. One can also find many Buddhist monasteries in Sikkim.
