Tour of Tamil Nadu - The Land of Vedas.

Tamil Nadu, literally The Land of Tamils, is one of the 29 states of India. The capital of Tamil Nadu is Chennai.

Location

Located in south India, TN is bound by Andhra Pradesh and Karnataka to the north, the Indian Ocean to the south, the Bay of Bengal to the east, and Kerala to the west.
