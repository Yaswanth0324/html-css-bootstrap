Kerala

Kerala is situated on the southwestern tip of India. It’s known as "God’s Own Country" for its beautiful backwaters and lush greenery.

Tamil Nadu

Tamil Nadu is in southern India, rich in culture, temples, and classical arts. It borders Kerala, Andhra Pradesh, and Karnataka.

Sikkim

Sikkim is in northeast India, known for its natural beauty, Buddhist monasteries, and alpine landscapes.
