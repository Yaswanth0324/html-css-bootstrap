Location

Located in south India, TN is bound by Andhra Pradesh and Karnataka in the north, Indian Ocean in the south, Bay of Bengal in the east, and Kerala in the west.

Fairs & Festivals

Pongal is the main festival in Tamil Nadu. Celebrated in January, it marks the harvest season. Thaipusam, celebrated in January and February in Thanjavur and Palani, is marked by ritual bathing.

Hill Stations in Tamil Nadu

Ooty
Known for valleys, wild flowers, lush forests, and tea plantations.
Kodaikanal
Surrounded by rocks and lakes, a global tourist attraction.
Yercaud
A beautiful mountain territory in Salem district.
